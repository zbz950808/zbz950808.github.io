About

Bozhou Zhuang is a Ph.D. researcher at Sonny Astani Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering, University of Southern California with Viterbi/Graduate School Fellowship. Prior to joining USC, he received his Master's degree in Civil Engineering from Tongji University, China. His research interests lie in multidiscipline fields of civil and electrical engineering, specifically on the spent nuclear fuels (SNFs) dry storage systems and structures, ultrasonic testing and signal processing, machine learning, steel & composite structures, bridge engineering.
