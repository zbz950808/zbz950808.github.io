About

Hello there! I'm Bozhou Zhuang [CV], a Ph.D. researcher at Sonny Astani Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering, University of Southern California. I am a recipient of multiple fellowships, including the Viterbi/Graduate School Fellowship ($38,000), Professor Teh-Fu Yen and Dr. Shia-Ping Siao Yen Fellowship in Civil and Environmental Engineering ($19,000) and Das Family Travel Award ($1,000). Prior to joining USC, I received my Master's degree in Civil Engineering from Tongji University, China. My research interests lie in multidiscipline fields of civil and electrical engineering, specifically on the spent nuclear fuels (SNFs) dry storage systems and structures, ultrasonic testing and signal processing, machine learning, steel & composite structures, bridge engineering. I am an Engineer-in-Training (EIT) in California.
